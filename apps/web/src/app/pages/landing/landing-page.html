About

Bank of Namibia

The Bank of Namibia is the central bank of the Republic of Namibia, established under Article 128 of the Namibian Constitution. It is responsible for promoting and maintaining a sound monetary, credit, and financial system in Namibia, and serves as fiscal adviser and banker to the Government.

The Initiative

What is Instant Payment Namibia?

Instant Payment Namibia (IPN) is a modernisation initiative led by the Bank of Namibia to bring real-time electronic payment capabilities to the nation. It enables Person-to-Person (P2P) transfers that settle instantly, replacing the delays of traditional batch-processed payments with immediate, round-the-clock transaction processing.
